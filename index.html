How to use

1) Pick the strategy

Turnkey for turnkey rentals, BRRRR for rehab + refi, and Flip for rehab + sale.

2) Enter assumptions

Use realistic rents, expenses, taxes/insurance, and financing terms.

3) Read the outputs

Compare cash flow, cash-on-cash, total cash needed, or profit metrics depending on the strategy.

4) Check “More Info”

Each analyzer links to definitions and notes tailored to that strategy.
